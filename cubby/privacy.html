Cubby — Privacy Policy

Effective: February 14, 2026

Overview

Cubby is designed with privacy at its core. Your inventory data stays on your device.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data. Cubby stores all item data, photos, and organizational information locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage.

Subscription Data

If you subscribe to Cubby Pro, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and subscription status — no personal information, inventory data, or device identifiers are shared. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

Cubby does not include any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Photos

Photos you add to items are stored locally on your device. They are never uploaded to any server.

Children's Privacy

Cubby does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
