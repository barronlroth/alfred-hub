Big Adventure
Pick one — full day Saturday commitment, worth every minute
✓
Tomales Bay: Elk Hike + Fresh Oysters
Hike through wildflower-covered bluffs to spot herds of tule elk roaming free, then reward yourselves with oysters fresh from Tomales Bay. Nature and seafood perfection.
⏱ Full day 🚗 1.5 hrs north 💡 Hike first, oysters after
✓
Mount Tamalpais Day Hike
The crown jewel of Marin hiking. Panoramic views from the summit — ocean, bay, and city all at once. Multiple trail options from mellow to legit. Bring layers, it gets windy up top.
⏱ Full day 🚗 45 min north 💰 Free
✓
Napa Valley Wine Country
World-class wineries, gorgeous rolling hills, incredible food. Hit 2–3 tastings, grab lunch in Yountville, and pretend you're on a European vacation. Full day, fully worth it.
⏱ Full day 🚗 1.5 hrs north 💰 $$–$$$
✓
Alamere Falls Hike
One of only two tidefalls in California — a waterfall that drops directly onto the beach and into the ocean. The hike through Point Reyes is beautiful, and the payoff is genuinely jaw-dropping. Bucket-list stuff.
⏱ Full day 🚗 1.5 hrs north 💰 Free 💡 ~8 mi round trip, moderate
✓
Pescadero Farm Loop
A little European countryside detour an hour south. Tour Harley Farms goat dairy, grab legendary artichoke bread from Arcangeli Grocery, explore tide pools at Pescadero State Beach. Charming and totally unique.
⏱ Half day 🚗 1 hr south 💰 $ 💡 Pair with Pigeon Point Lighthouse
✓
Half Moon Bay Coastal Day
Charming coastal town with dramatic cliffs, world-famous Mavericks surf break, great seafood, and a gorgeous Coastal Trail walk. Laid-back California vibes with real wow-factor scenery.
⏱ Full day 🚗 45 min south 💰 $–$$ 💡 Sam's Chowder House for lunch

City Exploration
Pick one — Friday, Saturday evening, or Sunday morning
✓
Bike the Golden Gate → Sausalito
Rent bikes, ride across the Golden Gate Bridge, explore the charming waterfront town of Sausalito, then ferry back. The quintessential SF experience — iconic for a reason.
⏱ Half day 💰 ~$40 bike rental 💡 Ferry back from Sausalito
✓
Lands End Trail
Gorgeous coastal trail with Golden Gate Bridge views, rugged cliffs, and the eerie ruins of Sutro Baths. Easy-moderate walk right in the city — stunning at any time of day. One of SF's best-kept secrets.
⏱ 1.5–2 hrs 🚶 Bus to trailhead 💰 Free
✓
Walk Around SF + Shopping
Explore SF's best neighborhoods on foot — Hayes Valley boutiques, Union Square flagship stores, Valencia Street's eclectic shops. Coffee stops, people-watching, and zero agenda required.
⏱ Half day 🚶 Walkable 💰 $–$$$
