The Concept

A blind date night,
every two weeks.

Alfred — Barron's AI butler — runs a personal dining concierge called Maître D'. He researches San Francisco's best restaurants, books the perfect spot, and keeps it a complete secret. Barron, Nina, Sarah, and Lukas receive only a Waymo pickup time. No restaurant name. No cuisine hint. They discover where they're going when the car pulls up.
