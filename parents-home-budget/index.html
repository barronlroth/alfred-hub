Recommendation
Gentle recommendation

Do not choose the $700k prebuild unless it can be made almost mortgage-free.

Otherwise it likely creates a monthly support need for the kids.

The $700k prebuild works only if…
  • ✓The family contributes or loans roughly $250k–$300k.
  • ✓The remaining mortgage is tiny.
  • ✓Delay risk and builder costs are understood.

  • ✓Keep looking under $500k all-in.
