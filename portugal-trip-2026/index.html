Practical Notes

Source of Truth

This page is the public-readable plan. TripIt, direct airline/vendor confirmations, and the Hermes trip memory remain canonical for exact times and booking details.

Flights

Out: SFO → JFK, TWA overnight, JFK → Madrid, Madrid → Lisbon. Back: Lisbon → JFK → SFO on May 25.

Rental Car

Pick up at Lisbon Airport on May 14 and return at Lisbon Airport on May 22. Main road-trip route is LIS → Comporta → Porto → Douro → LIS.

Still Worth Checking

TWA Hotel confirmation, JetBlue/Iberia live times, Canalha Comporta, Niepoort, Raiva, wedding venue/dress code, and Lisbon airport transfer timing.

May Weather

Expect warm days, cooler evenings, and Atlantic variability. Pack beach layers, dinner layers, comfortable walking shoes, and wedding-specific clothing separately.

Tolls

Portugal highways use electronic tolls. Make sure the rental has Via Verde or an equivalent toll setup before leaving Lisbon Airport.

Power

Type F European outlets, 230V. Bring adapters for phones, laptops, camera chargers, and whatever cable you will confidently forget otherwise.

Data

Use roaming or an eSIM/local SIM with enough data for Maps. The Douro is not the place to discover your phone plan is decorative.
