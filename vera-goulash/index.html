Slow beef · onions · Fant packet diplomacy

Do not turn it into soup.

Vera’s goulash is a patient, onion-rich beef braise: glossy onions, meat cooked low in its own juices, water added only by the splash, tomato sauce at the end, and Fant seasoning doing its quiet Balkan sorcery.

Deciphered from Vera

The recipe lives in the verbs.

The transcript is approximate, but the technique is clear: sauté, braise, reduce, add water reluctantly, and stop before it becomes soup.

Onions

“Three heads” means 3 medium yellow onions, diced and cooked until glossy.

Beef

“Kilogram and hungry grams” is roughly 1–1.1 kg, a little over two pounds of cubed stew meat.

Dinstati

A Balkan/Serbo-Croatian cooking verb: gently stew or braise, feeding the pot just enough liquid to keep going.

Ingredients

Short list. Long patience.

Nothing fussy: onions, beef, salt, tomato, water by the splash, and the seasoning packet that apparently has diplomatic immunity.

Thick, glossy, onion-rich sauce. Tender beef. No watery nonsense.

Method

Low heat, wooden spoon, no panic.

This is a dish you steer. Stir every 10–15 minutes, watch the liquid, and add water only when the pot asks for it.

  1. 01

    Cook the onions. Dice 3 medium yellow onions. Cook them in olive oil until glossy and softened.

  2. 02

    Add the beef. Stir cubed beef directly into the onions. Keep the heat very low.

  3. 03

    Braise in its own juices. The beef will release liquid. Let it cook there, stirring every 10–15 minutes.

  4. 04

    Add water reluctantly. Once the juices reduce, add water little by little. Do not fully submerge the meat.

  5. 05

    Cook until tender. Continue low braising until the beef starts falling apart. This can take 2.5–3 hours total.

  6. 06

    Finish the sauce. Stir in about 150 ml tomato sauce. Mix Fant seasoning with a little liquid, then stir it back into the pot.

  7. 07

    Adjust thickness. Add only tiny splashes of water if needed. Serve thick and saucy with noodles, potatoes, bread, or the correct thing Vera will eventually confirm.
